Who We Are

Creative Minds Behind Great Brands

Dadris Designers is a Kenya-based creative design studio committed to helping businesses of all sizes look professional, credible, and memorable. We believe every business deserves great design — and that design is not just about how things look, but how they work and how they make people feel.

From startups to established companies, we've helped over 80 clients across Kenya and beyond build brands that stand out. Whether you need a simple logo or a complete web presence, we bring the same level of passion and professionalism to every project.
